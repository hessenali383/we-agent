# WE Space Super (Fixed Internet Plans) - Telecom Egypt

## Overview
WE Space plans offer the most powerful home internet in Egypt with high speeds, large quotas, and entertainment content. The "Super" tier provides speeds up to 30 Mbps, along with multiple payment methods (monthly, annually), add-on recharge bundles, and additional Gaming bundles.

## Add-on Quotas
In case you consume your basic download quota, you will enjoy unlimited internet at a lower speed. To return to the original speed, you can either renew early or recharge any of the add-on quotas (valid for one time or renew automatically every 30 days).

### Add-on Quotas (One-time or Auto-renewal)
* **20 GB:** 60 EGP
* **50 GB:** 120 EGP
* **100 GB:** 190 EGP

### Add-on Quotas (One-time - Giga Tank)
* **400 GB:** 500 EGP
* **2,000 GB (2 TB):** 2,000 EGP

## Multiple Payment Methods (WE SPACE Annual Plans)
With annual plans, you will only pay the value of 10 months and enjoy internet service for a full year at a discount (you receive the total bundle quota upfront to use throughout the year).

### Annual Plans Quotas for Super Speed:
* Quota 3,000 GB (equivalent to 250 GB/month)
* Quota 4,800 GB (equivalent to 400 GB/month)
* Quota 9,000 GB (equivalent to 750 GB/month)
* Quota 18 TB (equivalent to 1500 GB/month)
