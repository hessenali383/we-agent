# Management Team - Telecom Egypt

## Tamer El-Mahdi - Managing Director and CEO
Eng. Tamer El-Mahdi was appointed as Managing Director of Telecom Egypt in September 2025 after joining the company's Board of Directors as a non-executive member representing the Egyptian government in March 2025.
With over 30 years of experience, Tamer El-Mahdi has served as the Founder and Chairman of Trade Globe and Terrawaves Networks since 2020, where he oversees global operations in telecommunications consulting, network design, and digital infrastructure development. His career includes numerous executive leadership positions in several multinational companies and investment firms across various sectors such as telecommunications, digital transformation, fintech, real estate, mining, and non-banking financial services.
From 2014 to 2019, he served as the CEO of Orascom Telecom Media and Technology (OTMT), where he led the strategic transformation and diversification process across multiple sectors and regions, including Europe, South Asia, and the Middle East. Prior to that, he held the position of CEO of Orascom Telecom Algeria (Djezzy), the largest mobile operator in Algeria, from 2008 to 2013. During his tenure at Orascom Telecom Holding, he held several key leadership positions, including Group Chief Technology Officer (2003-2008), where he managed multi-billion dollar investments and spearheaded mobile network launches in Canada, Bangladesh, Iraq, and North Korea.
Early in his career, El-Mahdi held leadership roles at AT&T, where he managed massive telecommunications infrastructure projects across North Africa.
Tamer El-Mahdi holds a Master's degree in International Business Administration from Maastricht School of Management, a Master's in Project Management from George Washington University, a Master's degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from California State University, and a Bachelor's degree in the same field from Cairo University.

## El-Husseiny Adel - Vice President for Regional Affairs
Eng. El-Husseiny Adel was appointed as Vice President for Regional Affairs in October 2023 after serving as Vice President for Customer Service in October 2021.
Adel served as Acting Chief Customer Officer since 2019. During that period, he was responsible for consolidating call center operations while introducing new methods to develop sales services and customer support to secure the company's various revenue streams. Concurrently, Eng. Adel was also appointed as the project manager for one of the company's most important major investment projects, "Egypt Internet," which involved accelerating the fiber rollout plan to reach 90% of customers during 2019 and 2020. Adel played a fundamental role in managing and executing the project, which improved fixed internet services nationwide in terms of speed and quality and paved the way for launching new products in the market.
Adel joined Telecom Egypt in 2007 as General Manager of Operations and over 14 years held several managerial positions in transmission networks and regional affairs. During that period, Adel was responsible for developing and managing network expansions by overseeing operational support to ensure the quality and availability of network services.
Eng. Adel holds a Bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering from Menoufia University and a Master's in Business Administration from the Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport.

## Ramy Kato - Vice President for Corporate and Enterprise Commercial Affairs
Ramy Kato joined Telecom Egypt in December 2023 as Vice President for Customer Service to lead and achieve the company's customer-centric goals. Kato possesses over 26 years of experience in customer service management in major companies across the Middle East, including telecommunications, financial services, e-commerce, digital payments, and energy companies. His expertise includes working with local and international startups, scaling operations, digital transformation, and achieving operational efficiency.
From March 2020 to December 2023, Kato served as the Head of the Egypt Customer Service Center at Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (_VOIS), where he achieved numerous accomplishments including developing and improving the _VOIS brand as an employer. He led the customer service transformation within _VOIS to build future customer service operations and oversaw customer service operations across Europe, Egypt, and India with over 8,000 permanent employees.
From October 2018 to February 2020, Kato worked as General Manager for Global Finance, a subsidiary of Global Leasing providing financial services. In September 2016, Kato served as Vice President of Careem Dubai before being appointed CEO of Careem Egypt, a ride-hailing startup offering services in 19 countries in the MENA region. Prior to joining Careem, Kato's career path led him to the position of CEO of Xceed, the leading outsourcing company in Egypt and North Africa, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Telecom Egypt.
Ramy Kato holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics from the American University in Cairo and a Master's in Business Administration from Rennes School of Business in France.

## Wael Hanafy - Chief Financial Officer
Mr. Wael Hanafy assumed the position of Chief Financial Officer in October 2024.
Hanafy joined Telecom Egypt in 2002 and held several positions until he served as Head of the Financial Affairs Sectors before assuming this role. He also led the Investor Relations team from 2022 to 2024, successfully enhancing investor confidence in the company, attracting further investments, and strengthening communication with all stakeholders.
His career at Telecom Egypt, spanning over 20 years, included managing several important financial portfolios, including corporate finance, investment strategies, financial planning, budgeting, and others. His broad and diverse knowledge, along with his practical approach, qualified him to successfully manage numerous complex mergers and acquisitions by ensuring clarity of vision, financial auditing, and accuracy in decision-making, which contributed to strengthening relationships with investors, telecom operators, and suppliers.
Wael Hanafy graduated from the Faculty of Commerce at Helwan University and holds a Certified Management Accountant (CMA) certification.

## Ismail Said - Chief Technical Officer
Ismail Said was appointed as Chief Technical Officer at Telecom Egypt in October 2025, bringing over 25 years of leadership experience in telecommunications, digital infrastructure, and technological innovation across the Middle East, Africa, and Europe.
Prior to his appointment to his new position, he served as Vice President of Network Planning and Development at GBI, where he led large-scale technology and infrastructure programs spanning over 45,000 kilometers of submarine fiber networks, managed multinational teams in 11 countries, and spearheaded the company's digital transformation and data center expansion.
He has held several executive positions, including Chief Information Technology Officer and Co-founder of Klivvr, one of Egypt's leading fintech companies, where he established several electronic payment and digital lending platforms. He also served as Vice President of Operations and Managed Services at E-Serve, overseeing major digital transformation projects in e-government and education.
During his previous tenure at Telecom Egypt as Vice President for Information Technology, Ismail led the implementation and launch of the 4G network, managed technology investments of over $400 million, and developed the first Egyptian cloud platform.
Said holds an MBA in Marketing from the Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport, and a Bachelor's degree in Telecommunications Engineering from Cairo University.
He is widely recognized for his role in bridging technology and business strategies, and for being a visionary, results-oriented leader driven by a pursuit of operational excellence and the delivery of innovative digital solutions on a large scale.

## Ahmed El-Fouly - Chief Information and Technology Officer
Ahmed El-Fouly was appointed as Chief Information and Technology Officer in February 2026. El-Fouly possesses over 25 years of global experience in telecommunications and IT, financial services technology, and digital transformation, gained through his work with major telecom companies in the Middle East and North Africa.
Prior to joining Telecom Egypt, Mr. Ahmed El-Fouly served as Vice President of Information Technology at Beyond One in Oman since October 2024. During this period, he led IT operations and mobile financial services, overseeing the launch of Friendi Pay, a money transfer service licensed by the Central Bank of Oman. He also led several key strategic initiatives, including the migration and transfer of Friendi subscribers to different service providers and achieving significant gains in operational efficiency through migrating to cloud computing, automation, and effectively utilizing AI capabilities.
From 2021 to 2024, El-Fouly served as Chief Technology and Information Systems Officer at Virgin Mobile Kuwait, where he led the launch of a fully digital Mobile Virtual Network Operator (MVNO) with two brands targeting different segments. He successfully developed a fully integrated Cloud-Native technology ecosystem enabling a seamless digital customer experience and enhancing operational integration and collaboration with STC.
Prior to that, he served as Head of the Telecommunications and Information Technology Sectors at Ooredoo Kuwait, leading major transformation programs such as launching the first app-based telecommunications service program in Kuwait and developing the 5G infrastructure. He also held leadership positions at Orascom Telecom (Djezzy/VEON) in Algeria and Vodafone Egypt, overseeing numerous large-scale network development, IT, and digital transformation projects across multiple markets.
El-Fouly holds a Master's in International Business Administration from ESLSCA University (Egypt/France) and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and Information Systems from Ain Shams University.

## Mohamed El-Touny - Vice President for Consumer Commercial Affairs
Mohamed El-Touny was appointed as Vice President for Consumer Commercial Affairs at Telecom Egypt in January 2026, bringing over 20 years of experience in leadership positions in the telecommunications industry across the Middle East, Africa, and Europe.
Throughout his career, El-Touny has led several commercial organizations, achieving sustainable growth through various strategies centered on customer experience, digital transformation, and commercial excellence.
