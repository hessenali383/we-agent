# WE Gold (WE Gold Plans)

## Overview of WE Gold Plans
WE Gold plans offer comprehensive benefits including mobile internet, calling minutes, fixed internet (or WE Air wireless), and landline, along with exceptional features such as device installments, Vezeeta's "Shamel" healthcare service, entertainment subscriptions (TOD), and an international travel experience.

### General Features for WE Gold Customers:
- **Control Your Bundle:** Local mobile minutes to the WE network or other networks are billed at a 1:1 ratio.
- **Flexible Consumption of Megabytes and Minutes:**
  - After consuming the mobile internet bundle, the minutes bundle can be used at a rate of (1 MB = 1 minute).
  - In case both the mobile internet and minutes bundles are consumed, the home internet bundle can be used at a rate of (1 mobile MB = 20 home internet MB).
- **Gigabyte Rollover:** Unused (mobile) gigabytes are rolled over to the next month.
- **WE Bonus Points:** The customer earns points that can be redeemed (every 100 points = 5 EGP). Points are valid for two years.

## Additional Services and Features

### "Shamel" Healthcare Subscription from Vezeeta
* Provides discounts up to 80% on clinic services, surgeries, tests, scans, and pharmacies (over 8,000 healthcare providers).
* Individual subscription is available for 300 and 600 plans.
* Family subscription is available for 890, 1200, 1500, 2300 plans.
* **Activation Method:** Send an SMS with the number (1) to the short code 111 and download the Vezeeta app. Validity: One year.

### TOD Entertainment Subscription
* Free subscription to watch sports matches (like the English Premier League and Champions League) and entertainment content.
* **Activation Method:** Send an SMS with the number (5) to 111 and follow the link to register using your WE Gold number.

### Travel & Roaming Experience
* **International Minutes:** Available starting from the 600 plan, and do not roll over to the next month. (Not available for hard-to-reach countries).
* **Roaming Internet:** Two bundles annually, validity of each bundle is one week. Can be activated via the My WE app or *800#.
* **WE Flight Service:** The 1500 and 2300 plans offer minutes to make calls during flights (on certain airlines) via the AeroMobile network. Activation via the My WE app or *800*11#.

### Family Sharing Plan
* Share your minutes and mobile internet with your family members (up to 6 members depending on the plan).
* Members can be added for 10 EGP per person, added to the invoice.
* An additional 10,000 minutes are granted for making calls between family members and the group owner.
* Add-on bundles cannot be shared. Activation and management via the My WE app or *800#.

### Device Voucher
* Starting from the 600 plan, the customer receives a one-time annual purchasing voucher (valid for one year) to buy devices or accessories from WE branches or B.TECH.
* Issued after paying 12 full invoices for new subscribers. It cannot be used to purchase a line or pay an invoice.

## Fixed Internet Upgrades
You can upgrade to a home internet bundle with a larger capacity and pay the difference with your monthly invoice:
* **To reach 200 GB Super:** Add 75 EGP (for 300 and 600 plans).
* **To reach 250 GB Super:** Add 145 EGP (for 300 and 600 plans), and free for 890 and 1200 plans.
* **To reach 400 GB Super:** Add 330 EGP (for 300 and 600 plans), add 185 EGP (for 890 and 1200 plans), and free for the 1500 plan.
* **To reach 750 GB Super:** Add 650 EGP (for 300 and 600 plans), add 505 EGP (for 890 and 1200 plans), add 320 EGP (for the 1500 plan), and free for the 2300 plan.
* **To reach 1500 GB Super:** Add 1070 EGP (for the 600 plan), and 750 EGP (for the 890 plan).
* **Note:** Home internet can be replaced with WE Air (SIM issuance fees apply). The WE Air bundle cannot be shared with family members.

## Device Installments
The possibility of paying for mobiles and WE Air / MiFi devices in installments over 12 months without interest from WE branches with a down payment ranging between 20% and 50%.
* **Current Customers (Indigo or Fixed Internet):** Benefit immediately upon switching to the service (subject to specific commitment conditions).
* **New Customers:** Benefit after 12 months for 300 and 600 plans, and after 6 months for the 890 plan and above.
* **WE Air and MiFi Devices:** WE Air installments start from 45 EGP, and MiFi installments start from 53 EGP.

## General Terms
* Prices are exclusive of VAT.
* The purchase price of a WE Gold line is 81.23 EGP for all plans.
* The invoice is issued on the 1st or 16th depending on the customer's billing cycle.
* The customer cannot downgrade the plan to any lower plan until a period (from 6 to 12 months) has passed.
* The customer bears the contracting expenses for home internet and landline.
* Out-of-bundle billing: 5 EGP per 200 MB (or choosing Nitro bundles), and 14 piasters per minute or SMS.
* Billing after consuming 140 directory: 1.50 EGP per minute.
