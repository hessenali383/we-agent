# WE Landline Prepaid Plans (WE Ardy)

## Overview and Payment Facilities
Telecom Egypt offers a prepaid system for landlines (WE Ardy) with features allowing customers to self-manage their landline, recharge their balance, monitor consumption, and control expenses through digital channels (My WE app or website).
The company provides flexible payment systems: **Monthly, Quarterly, or Annually**. When choosing the quarterly or annual payment method, you can utilize the total bundle minutes without them being distributed over the number of months.

---

## Details of Basic WE Ardy Plans

### 1. WE Ardy 35 Plan
* **Bundle Price:** 35 EGP/month (39.90 EGP including VAT and table tax).
* **Bundle Minutes:** 500 minutes monthly (local and governorates).
* **Value-Added Services:** Caller ID + Follow-Me (Call forwarding).
* **Payment System (excluding tax):**
  * Monthly: 35 EGP (500 minutes)
  * Quarterly: 105 EGP (1,500 minutes)
  * Annually: 420 EGP (6,000 minutes)

### 2. WE Ardy 45 Plan
* **Bundle Price:** 45 EGP/month (51.80 EGP including VAT and table tax).
* **Bundle Minutes:** 3,500 minutes monthly (local / governorates / WE Mobile).
* **Value-Added Services:** Caller ID + Follow-Me (Call forwarding).
* **Payment System (excluding tax):**
  * Monthly: 45 EGP (3,500 minutes)
  * Quarterly: 135 EGP (10,500 minutes)
  * Annually: 540 EGP (42,000 minutes)

### 3. WE Ardy 75 Plan
* **Bundle Price:** 75 EGP/month (88.70 EGP including VAT and table tax).
* **Bundle Minutes:** 3,500 minutes (local / governorates / WE Mobile) + 250 minutes (mobile to other networks).
* **Value-Added Services:** Caller ID + Follow-Me (Call forwarding).
* **Payment System (excluding tax):**
  * Monthly: 75 EGP
  * Quarterly: 225 EGP
  * Annually: 900 EGP

*(Note: Unused minutes within the bundle do not roll over to the next month).*

## Out-of-Bundle Billing and Salefny Service

### In Case Basic Minutes Run Out
If minutes run out and no early renewal or subscription to an add-on bundle is made, the tariff is:
* **Calls (local, governorates, WE mobile):** 3 piasters/minute + 10 piasters call setup fee.
* **Calls to other mobile networks / virtual landlines:** 14 piasters/minute + 10 piasters call setup fee.
*(An 8% table tax is added to the tariff for calls deducted from the balance).*

### Salefny Service (Borrow Service)
Grants 40 units for 2 EGP, valid for 30 days. It can be used once a month and can be subscribed to via the app, website, 111, or branches.

## Value-Added and Premium Landline Services
* **Caller ID:** Identify incoming phone numbers.
* **Follow-Me Feature:** Forward calls to another number.
* **Multi-party Call (Conference Call):** Conduct a single phone call with multiple people.
* **Hotline:** Set a direct contact number for emergencies.
* **Do Not Disturb:** Control the non-reception of incoming calls.
* **Speed Dial:** Quick access to services.
* **Call Waiting:** Receive an incoming call while on a current call.
* **Code Barring:** Control the making of certain types of outgoing calls.
* **Other Services:** Silver Bundle (discount on mobile calls), Preferred Numbers, Balance Transfer, Receive-Only Feature, Temporary Suspension, Line Ownership Transfer, and Changing or Moving the Landline Number.

---

## International Calls
* **One World System for International Calls:** Make international calls to Zones 1, 2, and 3 at a price of 8 EGP + 1.50 EGP call setup fee.
* **Basic Tariff (per minute all day):**
  * Zones 1, 2, and 3: 10 EGP.
  * Zone 4 (Hard to reach): 30 EGP.
  * Zone 5 (Hard to reach): 45 EGP.
* **Satellite Calling:** Billed according to the monthly exchange rate of the dollar. (Inmarsat: 5 USD + 1 EGP, Iridium: 6.75 USD + 1 EGP, Thuraya: 2 USD + 1 EGP).

## Disconnection and Suspension Rules (Bundle Non-Renewal)
In case the main bundle is not renewed on time, the following rules apply based on the payment system:
* **Grace Period (Receive only):**
  * Monthly System: None.
  * Quarterly System: 3 days starting from the day following the renewal date.
  * Annual System: 7 days starting from the day following the renewal date.
* **Temporary Suspension (for the line):**
  * Monthly System: The line is suspended starting from the day following the renewal date.
  * Quarterly System: Suspension starts from the 4th day.
  * Annual System: Suspension starts from the 8th day.
*(Note: The line is temporarily suspended for up to 90 days, followed by permanent termination of the service).*
