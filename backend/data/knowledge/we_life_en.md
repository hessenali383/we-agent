# WE Life (Integrated Entertainment Plans) - Telecom Egypt

## Overview
The **WE LIFE** plan offers the best internet service, a landline subscription, and unlimited entertainment channels (IPTV), along with subscriptions to content streaming platforms, all in one bill.

---

## WE LIFE Plans Features
* **Unlimited Consumption (for Live Channels):** WE LIFE plans will not consume your home internet quota when watching live IPTV channels (like beIN and OSN).
* **Separate Streaming Quota:** You receive a free "streaming" quota to watch platforms like (Netflix, Prime Video, Apple TV, OSN+, etc.) upon subscription.
* **Multi-screen Viewing:** You can watch on multiple screens using a single account.
* **Landline:** All WE LIFE plans include a subscription to the **WE Ardy 45** landline plan.
* **Discount on Device (STB):** 50% discount on the Set-Top box device (for plans from 325 up to 1250), while it is offered as a gift in the 1600 and 2500 plans. The original price of the device is 1350 EGP (excluding tax).
* **Gifts:** The customer receives a gift purchasing voucher upon subscription, and the value of the voucher is roughly equivalent to the plan's value.
