## Fiber Plans

### WE Fiber Basic
- Speed: 25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload
- Monthly price: 250 EGP
- Fair usage: unlimited data, no throttling
- Best for: light browsing, streaming on one device, video calls

### WE Fiber Plus
- Speed: 100 Mbps download / 40 Mbps upload
- Monthly price: 400 EGP
- Fair usage: unlimited data, no throttling
- Best for: households with 3–5 connected devices, HD/4K streaming, gaming

### WE Fiber Pro
- Speed: 300 Mbps download / 100 Mbps upload
- Monthly price: 650 EGP
- Fair usage: unlimited data, no throttling
- Best for: large households, multiple 4K streams, remote work, competitive gaming

## ADSL Plans (legacy copper lines)

### WE ADSL Standard
- Speed: up to 8 Mbps download / 1 Mbps upload
- Monthly price: 150 EGP
- Available in areas without fiber coverage yet

## Add-ons

- **Static IP add-on**: +50 EGP/month, useful for hosting, remote access, or security cameras.
- **Extra router (mesh unit)**: +75 EGP one-time setup, recommended for homes larger than 150 sqm.
- **Priority support add-on**: +30 EGP/month, guarantees a callback within 2 hours instead of 24 hours.

## Switching or upgrading a plan

Customers can upgrade or downgrade their plan once per billing cycle with no
penalty. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle;
upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated on the next invoice.
