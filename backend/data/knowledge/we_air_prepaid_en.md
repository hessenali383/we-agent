# WE Air Prepaid

## Overview
The WE Air router is a portable internet broadcasting device that works in all coverage areas in Egypt using WE's 4G or 5G network.
Subscription to WE Air bundles starts from 290 EGP, and the WE Air service offers the largest home wireless internet bundles in Egypt with a capacity of up to 500 GB and best speeds reaching up to 400 Mbps.
The service provides 5G home wireless routers, and routers can be purchased in installments with partners like valU and Halan, in addition to a special 12-month interest-free installment plan for WE Gold customers.

## Add-on Bundles
You can subscribe to add-on bundles as needed (there is no maximum limit). The add-on bundle is valid for 30 days from the subscription date and does not renew automatically.
You can subscribe through the MY WE app or the router's main page.

## FAQs & Service Details

### Specifying Operating Location
* You can use the bundles (WE Air 290 and WE Air 460) in **only one operating location**. You can pay 46 EGP to obtain an additional operating location.
* As for other bundles, the device can be used in **two operating locations** (alternatively).
* The operating location is specified via the website, branches, or by calling 111. If the service is operated in an unregistered location, the service is suspended until you call and specify the location.

### Billing and Carry-over System
* **Bundle Renewal:** Main bundles renew automatically every 30 days if there is sufficient balance.
* **Consumption:** 1 MB is deducted from the bundle for every 1 MB of usage for all websites.
* **Upon consuming the main bundle:** You can early renew the same bundle, change to another bundle, or subscribe to add-on bundles.
* **Carry-over:** The carry-over service for remaining megabytes is available for main bundles only (provided there is no delay in the automatic renewal date or upon early renewal via the My WE app).

### Service Management
* To control the bundle, you must download the MY WE app and register with the data SIM number. The activation code message can be received on the router's page.
* The bundle can also be managed by dialing *979# when inserting the SIM into a mobile device.
* **Required Documents:** National ID card for Egyptians.
