## Additional Service Details

### How WE Air Postpaid Bundles Work
* Fixed monthly payment: A monthly invoice is issued on a fixed date, and you can pay it through any WE branch or via the My WE app.
* Megabyte carry-over: Any remaining megabytes from your main bundle will be carried over to the next month and will be consumed after using the new bundle.
* Add-on bundles: Fees for any add-on bundles you subscribe to during the month will be added to your upcoming monthly invoice.

### Available Routers

### FAQs and Usage Details
* **Required Documents:** National ID card (for Egyptians) and proof of Ability to Pay (ATP) or a commitment form.
* **Operating Location:** Bundles 690 and above allow the router to be used in two registered locations. Bundles 290 and 460 work in a single location, and an additional location can be added for 46 EGP annually.
* **Upon consuming the main bundle:** You can switch to a different bundle or subscribe to an add-on bundle via the app.
* **If you leave the 5G coverage area:** The device automatically switches to the best available network (4G or 3G).
