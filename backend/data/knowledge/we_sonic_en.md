# WE Sonic (Gaming and Streaming Plans) - Telecom Egypt

## Overview
**WE SONIC** plans offer the best gaming and streaming experience by providing the maximum possible line speed during gaming (with the highest upload and lowest lag). The plan provides dedicated gigabytes for gaming or streaming in addition to a basic quota for free usage. When playing games or using streaming services, consumption is calculated at double the capacity (X2 gigabytes), and if the gaming/streaming quota runs out, consumption is deducted from the basic quota.

---

## Basic WE SONIC Plans Details

### 1. GAMERZ Plan
* **Price:** 650 EGP/month
* **Speed:** Up to 80 Mbps (Maximum line speed is available during gaming for download and upload).
* **Highest Basic Download Speed:** 25 Mbps.
* **Total Quota:** Up to 500 GB.
  * Basic Quota: 250 GB.
  * Gaming Quota: 125 GB (Reaches up to 250 GB when the X2 feature is applied).
* **Unlimited Music and Meetings:** Includes (Soundcloud, Deezer, Anghami & Spotify) and (Microsoft Teams, Zoom & Google Meet).

### 2. STREAMERZ Plan
* **Price:** 650 EGP/month
* **Speed:** Up to 80 Mbps.
* **Highest Basic Download Speed:** 25 Mbps.
* **Total Quota:** Up to 500 GB.
  * Basic Quota: 200 GB.
  * Streaming Quota: 150 GB (Reaches up to 300 GB when the X2 feature is applied).
* **Unlimited Music and Meetings:** Includes (Soundcloud, Deezer, Anghami & Spotify) and (Microsoft Teams, Zoom & Google Meet).

### 3. PLUS Plan
* **Price:** 1400 EGP/month
* **Speed:** Up to 150 Mbps (Maximum line speed is available during gaming for download and upload).
* **Highest Basic Download Speed:** 50 Mbps.
* **Total Quota:** Up to 1,500 GB.
  * Basic Quota: 500 GB.
  * Gaming and Streaming Quota: 500 GB (Reaches up to 1,000 GB when the X2 feature is applied).
* **Unlimited Music and Meetings:** Includes (Soundcloud, Deezer, Anghami & Spotify) and (Microsoft Teams, Zoom & Google Meet).

## Consumption Calculation (X2 Gigabytes Feature)
WE Sonic plans provide a double consumption feature for the gigabytes dedicated to gaming and streaming:

* **In GAMERZ Plan:** All games are included (PUBG, Asphalt9, Gameloft, FIFA, EA sports, EPIC, Riot, League of legends, Call of duty, Valorant and more) on all gaming devices.
* **In STREAMERZ Plan:** (Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, Prime video, Apple TV, Shahid, OSN plus, Snapchat).
* **In PLUS Plan:** Includes the features of both GAMERZ and STREAMERZ plans, in addition to (TikTok & Netflix).

## Add-on Quotas
If the customer exhausts the basic download quota, they can request early renewal, or recharge add-on quotas (one-time or automatically renewing every 30 days). The remaining internet quota does not roll over to the next month.

### Add-on Quotas (One-time or Auto-renewal)
* **20 GB:** 60 EGP
* **50 GB:** 120 EGP
* **100 GB:** 190 EGP

### Add-on Quotas (One-time - Giga Tank)
* **400 GB:** 500 EGP
* **2,000 GB (2 TB):** 2000 EGP

*(All prices are exclusive of VAT. Balance can be recharged through the My WE app, the website, or through authorized payment outlets like Fawry and Masary).*
