# WE Home Internet Services Overview - Telecom Egypt

WE network offers three main categories of home fixed internet services to suit the needs of different users:

## 1. WE Space (Basic Internet)
* **Target Audience:** For customers who prefer to use their internet quota as they wish.
* **Description:** Offers a range of bundles with download quotas up to 1500 GB, and various speeds up to 500 Mbps through monthly plans. It also provides the option to pay annually with a discount on the subscription.

## 2. WE Life (Integrated Entertainment Plans)
* **Target Audience:** For customers who prefer to have one consolidated bill.
* **Description:** Offers the best internet service, landline subscription, and unlimited entertainment channels (IPTV), all in one bill.

## 3. WE Sonic (Gaming and Live Streaming Plans)
* **Target Audience:** For customers looking for a unique and optimized experience in Gaming and Streaming.
* **Description:** Gives you double the gigabytes (2x) in gaming and streaming, in addition to a basic quota for normal usage. The bundle guarantees maximum internet speed with the highest Upload and lowest Lag during gameplay, providing the maximum possible line speed on any gaming device.
